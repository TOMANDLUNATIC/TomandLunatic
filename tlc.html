About TLC

TLC (Tom and Lunatic Championship) is not just a tournament, it’s a weekly championship. Players compete in organized matches, enjoy live commentary, and track weekly rankings.

TLC Official Rules

1. Server & Lag
All matches will be hosted on the Indian (Mumbai) server.
Teams from other regions are welcome, but must manage their own ping/lag issues. TL is not responsible for connection problems.

2. Match Setup
All matches will be played in a custom lobby with 10 bans (4 bans in WhatsApp group + 6 bans in-game). Teams must adapt to ping issues; no host advantage will be provided.

3. Punctuality
Punctuality is important. We will wait for teams to arrive. There is no fixed time limit, but a patience limit applies.

4. Player Conduct
Players must maintain respect and avoid toxic or harmful language. Recall spam is allowed, but sportsmanship and respect are required.

5. Team Names & Logos
Team names, logos, and profile pictures must not contain profanity or offensive content. (Does not apply to player names.)

6. Team Changes
Teams may update rosters weekly. Maximum 2 player substitutions per day. A player cannot play for more than one team on the same day.

7. Pause Rules
Each team is allowed 1 pause per match. Pause requests must be made in the WhatsApp group, not in-game.

8. Draft Issues & Disqualification Disputes
Issues like wrong draft, wrong pick, or time disqualification should be discussed by both team captains in the WhatsApp group. Following rules strictly is not unsportsmanlike.

9. Commentary
Commentary will primarily be in Tamil, occasionally English.

10. Rules & Penalties
Violations or misconduct may result in temporary or permanent bans from the tournament.

11. Rewards
TL provides ₹25 for each BO1 win and ₹100 for each BO3 win. Any additional gifts from sponsors or fans will also be distributed to players.

12. Defending Champions
Champions must defend their title weekly or bi-weekly to retain status.
